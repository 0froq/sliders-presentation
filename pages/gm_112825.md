## Breakpoint(s?) Detection

> with ERA5-Land Lake Mix-layer Temperature

---
layout: two-cols-header
---

- [algorithm / parameter]{.text-blue-500} sensitive
- massive differences in different algorithms

::left::

Parameters:
- Min segment length
- Max number of breakpoints
- Significance level
- \<Penalty type\>

::right::

Algorithms:
- PELT(Pruned Exact Linear Time)
- BinSeg(Binary Segmentation)
- SegNeigh(Segment Neighbourhoods)
- Rank
- Cusum(Cumulative Sum)
- Ftest
- MACD(Moving Average Convergence Divergence)
- Wavelet
- KNN
- Piecewise Linear Regression
